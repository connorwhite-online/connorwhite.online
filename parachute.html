Parachute Papers

Looking to transition out of skateboarding, David Gravette wanted to create a no-frills rolling paper company. He was attatched to the name Parachute, and I naturally saw a fit for a subscription model that would be our 'parachute supply drop'. Thus Parachute Papers was born. I created the identity, packaging, website, and style guide. I also had a chance once again to put more fully-compostable packaging out into the world.
