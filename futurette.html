Futurette

A project in its infancy, Futurette is home to the first 100% recycled polyester garments ever created. Utilizing SLS 3D-printing to prototype rPET garment hardware and aluminum heat-press tooling, we're nearing the release of the first 100% monocomponent garment, and also the first 100% recycled garment. I can't show the flagship pant or jacket at this point, but updates are on the way soon.
